How to Extract files - rar , zip , 7z...

There are many people out there who don't know to extract rar or zip files & some people don't  know the easiest way to do it,  So thats why we are here. First these rar, zip or 7z files are used to convert a group of files into just 1 file & these programs are called compression programs but why do we need them, We can easily group our files using folder, Yes we can but only inside our PC not on the Internet. You can't download a folder from the Internet without these compressing programs, without these you have to download each file 1 by 1 & thats is so difficult.
